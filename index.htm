Dupixent TVC:

A visually stylized approach

This TVC for Dupixent, a medication targeting resistant asthma, is about a point a view. Thanks to every trick of the trade (rig to talent, helmet, cams, lens, jibs),  the viewer experiences visually the freedom that comes from the medication: a full spectrum of physical activities! And the resulting visuals are striking.

Dupixent TVC specs:

Client/Product: Dupixent 
Production House: Splendors
Production Services: PSP - Production Services Portugal
Agency: Arnold
Director: James Lawes
DOP: Tim Fok

Shoot: 3 videos of 15 sec to 1 min, 8 days shoot around Lisbon

Special challenge:

47 roles in this cast: adults, children, every ethnicity.
Locations around Lisbon: Waterslide, water diving, pickle ball, playground, monkey bars, soccer field, supermarket, ext.house, docks, park, outdoor trail, bedroom.

Centrum TVC

​

Centrum´s campaign promotes their product as something for everybody, including a 50+ adults version. Thanks to the TVCs´ informal and upbeat mood, everyone can identify with its jingle: You did it! You did it!

We did it together: a high energy shoot with a wonderful talent pool of women (director, DP, production designer, and...us!)

We did it on the road: We put together a very diverse cast, and shot in upbeat locations: playground, city park, gym, pool, and beach, interiors.

We faked it: snow in middle of summer!

We did it in fives: 5 videos (between 5 and 15 seconds) in 5 days.
